SolarQuant Documentation¶

The SolarQuant environment tooling allows the user to easily source training data to be used for training machine learning predictors. SolarQuant works by consuming SolarNetwork [1] and OrangeButton [2] data, both of which are free and open source.

Note

SolarQuant is under active development, and the technologies involved are too.
